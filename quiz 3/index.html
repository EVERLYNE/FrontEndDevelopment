SOFA-SETS

about sofa-sets

This is an item of furniture for lying or sleeping. Sofa come in 3 major sizes. The full sofa is 84 (2.13m) wide. smaller versions like two sitter love seat range. Variations on the standard sofa include modular items and sofas with special uses such as daybeds. The style of the sofa is generally set by its arms which doubles as artistics statements and rests. The sofas are made most often from wood though other options include, steel, plastic and laminated bonds. A sofa maybe covered with any choice of synthetic natural or blended fabric.The designers makes sofas frame mostly from wood.After that they do padding which is primarily made from animals hair particularly horse.
